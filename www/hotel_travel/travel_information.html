Travel Information

To and From Airport

Xi’an Xianyang International Airport
Hotel direction: 44.3km SE
This hotel does not provide shuttle service.

Transportation:

  • Airport bus

    Ticket Offices:
    Terminal 2—Passenger Service Center (next to Exit 2)
    Terminal 3—Located in the Arrival Hall and in the Ground Transportation Center (GTC)

    Location of Platforms:
    Terminal 2—After leaving the baggage claim area, please proceed to Exit 2 and make your way across to
    the platforms.
    Terminal 3—After leaving the baggage claim area, please proceed to Exit 314 and make your way to the
    Ground Transportation Center (GTC) .Then please continue to the bus boarding area in the back right
    (south-west) corner of the building.

    Airport Bus Route:
    From Xi’an Xianyang International Airport
    To Xibei Hotel

    Bus Service Fee: 25 RMB (one way)
    No reservation required
    After you arrived at Xibei Hotel, you may transfer to taxi (16 RMB) or bus Travel 9 or 320 (2 RMB, Miao Po
    Tou Bus Stop) to Wyndham Grand.

  • Taxi

    Estimated Taxi Fee: 125 RMB (one way)
